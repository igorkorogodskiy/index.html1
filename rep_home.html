My favourite team in Formula 1

My favourite team in Formula 1 is McLaren

Team website

McLaren is one of the most successful teams in F1.

After a decline in 2012, the team returned to victory only in 2024, after 12 unsuccessful years.

They owe this not only to good engineers and mechanics, but also to a young and talented team.

This year, the team is fighting for 2 titles, the Constructors' Cup and the F1 Driver's Cup.

While the Constructors Cup has a chance of winning at almost 100%, the Drivers Cup is still undecided.

They are up against another talented driver, a 3-time F1 champion.

Team composition for the 2024 season
  • Lando Norris(Car number 4)
  • Oscar Piastri(Car number 81)
  • Pato O'Ward (reserve driver)
